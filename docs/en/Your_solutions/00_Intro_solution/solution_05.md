## 5. Trigonometry
**Problem:** A vector has a magnitude of 15 and makes an angle of $60^\circ$ with the positive x-axis. Find its horizontal ($A_x$) and vertical ($A_y$) components.

* **Step 1 (Horizontal Component):** We use the cosine function to find the x-component:
  $A_x = A \cdot \cos(\theta) = 15 \cdot \cos(60^\circ)$
  $A_x = 15 \cdot 0.5 = 7.5$

* **Step 2 (Vertical Component):** We use the sine function to find the y-component:
  $A_y = A \cdot \sin(\theta) = 15 \cdot \sin(60^\circ)$
  $A_y = 15 \cdot \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2} \approx 12.99$

* **Final Answer:** The components are $A_x = 7.5$ and $A_y \approx 12.99$.
